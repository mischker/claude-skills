# Pay Less Now or Pay More Later — Attraction Offer

## Overview

Give customers two options: pay full price later (conditional on results / guarantee) OR pay a discounted price now and get additional bonuses. The pay-now discount plus bonuses makes immediate payment the obvious choice.

**Core mechanic:** Anchors full price as the "default," then rewards speed of commitment with savings + extras.

## The Two Options

**Option A — Pay More Later (Conditional)**
> "You can pay $[full price] after you see results. We start now, you pay when we hit [goal]."

This works because outcome-based pricing signals confidence. Used as the anchor.

**Option B — Pay Less Now (Immediate discount + bonuses)**
> "Or, pay $[discounted price] today and we'll also include [bonus 1], [bonus 2], and [bonus 3]. Most people choose this — you lock in [X]% savings and get more."

The bonus stack makes Option B irresistible vs. Option A.

## Pricing

- **Pay-now discount:** 20–50% off full price
- **Full price later:** only triggered if results are achieved (low risk for customer)
- Rule: Pay-now price must still cover 30-day costs

## Cancellation Threshold

- If > 10% cancel before paying full price → fix the product or adjust pricing
- High cancellation = the product isn't delivering the promised result, not a pricing problem

## Presentation Script

> "You've got two options. Option A: you pay nothing now, and once we hit [goal] together, you pay $[full price]. Option B: pay $[discounted price] today — that's [X]% off — and I'll throw in [bonus 1], [bonus 2], and [bonus 3] as a thank you for committing now. Which works better for you?"

Then stop talking. Let them choose.

## Bonus Stack for Pay-Now

The bonuses must be:
- Related to the core offer
- Independently valuable (priced items, not fluff)
- Things you already produce (no extra cost to deliver)

Good bonus types: templates, past recordings, group access, extra sessions, tools, resources.

## When to Use This vs. Other Attraction Offers

| Use this when... | Use something else when... |
|---|---|
| High-ticket, relationship-based offer | Mass-market / e-commerce product |
| Customer needs to trust you first | Customer already sold on the category |
| You have strong outcome confidence | No clear measurable outcome to defer payment to |

## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Fix |
|---|---|
| No bonuses for pay-now → not compelling | Add 3+ real-value bonuses to pay-now option |
| Pay-now discount < 20% → weak incentive | Minimum 20% off; 30–50% is stronger |
| Cancellation > 10% | Fix product delivery, not pricing |
| Full-price option feels fake | Make Option A real — some customers will choose it |
