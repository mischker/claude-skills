# Money Model Selector — Build Your $100M Money Model

## Overview

A money model is a deliberate sequence of offers. Build it one stage at a time. Each stage must work before adding the next.

**3 Stages:**
1. **Get Cash** → Attraction Offer (turns strangers into customers)
2. **Get More Cash** → Upsell + Downsell (makes more from each customer)
3. **Get The Most Cash** → Continuity Offer (keeps them paying)

**Target:** Revenue from one customer > cost to get + serve that customer within 30 days.

---

## Step 1: Identify Business Type

Answer these questions first:

**What do you sell?**
- [ ] Physical product
- [ ] Digital product / course / content
- [ ] Service (done-for-you)
- [ ] Software / SaaS
- [ ] Local service business

**What's the outcome customers want?**
Write one sentence: "Customers want to ______ without ______."

**What's the core trust challenge?**
- [ ] "I don't know if I can afford it"
- [ ] "I don't know if it'll work for me"
- [ ] "I don't know if I'll use it"
- [ ] "I've been burned before"

---

## Step 2: Select Attraction Offer

Pick ONE. Master it before adding more.

| Offer | Best for | Core mechanic |
|---|---|---|
| **Win Your Money Back** (`mm-win-your-money-back`) | High-engagement services (fitness, coaching, programs) | Pay upfront; get money back if goals are hit |
| **Giveaway** (`mm-giveaway`) | Lead generation, e-commerce, local businesses | Prize attracts leads; non-winners get a promotional offer |
| **Decoy Offer** (`mm-decoy-offer`) | Consulting, coaching, software, B2B | Free DIY option displayed next to premium paid option |
| **Buy X Get Y Free** (`mm-buy-x-get-y-free`) | Physical products, subscriptions | More free than paid; prepaid customers keep buying |
| **Pay Less Now** (`mm-pay-less-now`) | High-ticket services, transformations | Pay full price later (conditional) OR discounted now with bonuses |

**Quick match:**
- "People don't trust us yet" → **Giveaway** or **Win Your Money Back**
- "People know what they want but think it's too expensive" → **Pay Less Now** or **Decoy**
- "People need to try before they commit" → **Decoy**
- "Physical product; need volume" → **Buy X Get Y Free**

---

## Step 3: Select Upsell Offer

Pick ONE. Offer it immediately after purchase.

| Offer | Best for | Core mechanic |
|---|---|---|
| **Classic Upsell** (`mm-classic-upsell`) | Any business with a clear "next problem" | Solve the immediate next problem right after purchase |
| **Menu Upsell** (`mm-menu-upsell`) | Multiple products; in-person or call-based sales | Unsell, prescribe, A/B, card on file |
| **Anchor Upsell** (`mm-anchor-upsell`) | When you suspect larger budgets; high-ticket | Premium first, main offer looks like a deal |
| **Rollover Upsell** (`mm-rollover-upsell`) | Win-back campaigns; upset customers; short-to-long commitment | Credit past purchase toward a bigger next one |

**Quick match:**
- "Just sold first offer; what's next?" → **Classic Upsell**
- "Have lots of products to sell at once" → **Menu Upsell**
- "Leaving money on the table from rich buyers" → **Anchor Upsell**
- "Customers churning after short commitment" → **Rollover Upsell**

---

## Step 4: Select Downsell Offer(s)

Use these when the customer says no. Combine them — alternate between payment plan and feature downsells.

| Offer | Use when | Core mechanic |
|---|---|---|
| **Payment Plan Downsell** (`mm-payment-plan-downsell`) | "I can't afford it upfront" | Same price, different timing |
| **Trial With Penalty** (`mm-trial-with-penalty`) | All else fails; recurring product | Free if they do the work; pay if they don't |
| **Feature Downsell** (`mm-feature-downsell`) | "I don't need everything" | Remove features, lower price |

**Standard sequence:** Payment Plan → (if desire is 8+, keep going) → Feature Downsell → (alternate) → Trial With Penalty

---

## Step 5: Select Continuity Offer

Add this LAST. Only after attraction offer generates reliable customers.

| Offer | Best for | Core mechanic |
|---|---|---|
| **Continuity Bonus** (`mm-continuity-bonus`) | Most businesses | Valuable bonus for signing up to membership today |
| **Continuity Discount** (`mm-continuity-discount`) | Services with contracts, subscriptions | Free time given up front, at end, or spread over commitment |
| **Waived Fee** (`mm-waived-fee`) | High-ticket B2B, long-term services | Fee waived for year commitment; fee charged for month-to-month |

## Example Money Models

**Fitness / Coaching (Service)**
1. Attraction: Win Your Money Back
2. Upsell: Menu Upsell (supplements / add-ons)
3. Downsell: Payment Plan → Trial With Penalty
4. Continuity: Continuity Discount (lifetime rate after 12 months)

**Consulting / Agency (B2B)**
1. Attraction: Decoy Offer (free audit + paid implementation)
2. Upsell: Anchor Upsell (done-for-you premium tier)
3. Downsell: Feature Downsell (DFY → DWY → DIY)
4. Continuity: Waived Fee Offer

**E-Commerce (Physical Product)**
1. Attraction: Buy X Get Y Free (bulk + auto-renewal)
2. Upsell: Classic Upsell (complementary products)
3. Downsell: Feature Downsell (smaller quantity)
4. Continuity: Continuity Bonus (subscription + monthly perks)

**SaaS / Software**
1. Attraction: Decoy Offer (free tier + paid tier)
2. Upsell: Classic Upsell (integrations, seats, features)
3. Downsell: Trial With Penalty (free trial with onboarding requirements)
4. Continuity: Continuity Discount (annual vs. monthly)

---

## Build Sequence Rules

1. **One offer at a time** — don't implement all stages at once; it will break your business
2. **Measure in quarters** — each stage takes time to become reliable
3. **Affiliate products can fill gaps** — sell someone else's complementary product for commission if you don't have an upsell yet
4. **Raise prices in stages** — start low for feedback, raise until you stop making more money
5. **Simple scales; fancy fails** — more ways to offer the same product, not more products
