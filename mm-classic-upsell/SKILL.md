# Classic Upsell — Upsell Offer

## Overview

The moment a customer buys, they immediately face a new problem. Offer them the solution to that next problem right then. "You can't have X without Y."

**Core mechanic:** Every purchase creates a new problem. Solve the next one immediately at the moment of peak motivation (right after they bought).

## How to Identify the Next Problem

Ask: "After a customer buys [main offer], what's the first thing that prevents them from succeeding with it?"

The answer is your classic upsell.

| Main offer | Next problem | Classic upsell |
|---|---|---|
| Gym membership | No meal plan | Nutrition coaching |
| Email tool | No email copy | Email template pack |
| Business course | No accountability | Mastermind access |
| Software license | No setup help | Onboarding service |
| Dog food | No supplements | Joint/vitamin pack |

## Timing

**Offer immediately after purchase** — at the moment of peak motivation, before buyer's remorse, before they leave.

In-person: right at the register / end of close.
Online: order confirmation page or post-purchase email sequence (within minutes).
Sales call: at the end of the close call before hanging up.

## Script

> "Before you go — [product] gets you [result]. But the #1 thing that prevents people from [getting full result] is [problem]. That's why I want to offer you [upsell] right now. Most people who get [main offer] also get [upsell] because [short reason]. Want to add it?"

Or harder close:
> "You can't really get [full result] without [upsell]. I'd feel bad letting you walk out without it."

## Pricing

- Classic upsell should be 25–100% of the main offer price
- Must be immediately usable and relevant
- The upsell should be a logical, obvious next step — not a random add-on

## Rules

- Offer once, clearly. Don't beg.
- You lose nothing by offering — worst case they say no
- Charge for guarantees/warranties if your business model supports it (turns a cost into revenue)
- Have as many classic upsells as you have sequential problems to solve

## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Fix |
|---|---|
| Upsell is unrelated to core offer | Must solve the customer's next logical problem |
| Offered too late (days after purchase) | Must be immediate — within minutes of purchase |
| Too many upsells at once | One upsell at a time; solve one next problem |
| Soft offer → easy to skip | Make it clear they need it; frame as essential |
